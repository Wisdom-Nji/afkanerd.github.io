We are a developing community of hackers, researchers, developers, designers and bloggers! We brand ourselves Afkanerd as meaning nerds from Africa.
Our mission is to use intelligence and technology to eradicate (solve) hard environmental, health, social and educational problems. This makes our mission a hard one too and thus achieving our mission is our very first vision. You do not really make a lot of money when you're working for the greater good of all mankind, but atleast you make greater change.

We outsouce our expertise to those willing to pay for it:
  • Android app development
  • API design and implementation
  • Database administration and design
  • Desktop application
  • Application customization
  • Code documentation
  • Server operational automations

    • We also do more computer engineering advanced jobs like:
  • Implementing machine learning to solve hard problems
  • Customizing source codes for hardware peripherals
  • Development of AI agents to tackle trivial task

    • With enough resources we also provide:
  • Security auditing and Penetration testing
